WHAT IS THIS EXPERTISE? WHY DOES IT MATTER?

Partners in social change convene at critical moments in the evolution of initiatives to learn from one another, exchange ideas, and affirm progress.  Conferences, meetings, and other gatherings of individuals and organizations are important opportunities for deepening and expanding the value of every participant’s work. 

The Principal of the T. L. Hill Group, Talmira Hill, has contributed as a speaker, presenter, and moderator to several events addressing youth development, education, youth workforce development, and young adult transition issues. 

TYPES OF PRODUCTS
