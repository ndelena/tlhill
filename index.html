The T. L. Hill Group works with nonprofit & philanthropic organizations and partnerships

  • To chart simple yet elegant, results-oriented courses of action that engage multiple partners in collaborating to achieve success;
  • To tap into the culture, perspectives, and resources of all partners engaged in an initiative to find and enhance solutions; and
  • To review, analyze, synthesize, and document milestones in the evolution of a social change initiative.

Ideas lead to successful results when customized management tools and information resources strengthen your ability to implement them. The T. L. Hill Group works with nonprofit and philanthropic organizations to design high quality initiatives that improve education, workforce, and youth development outcomes in low-income and underrepresented communities.
